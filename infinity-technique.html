Introducing The Infinity Technique

Welcome back, avid readers! In this session, we're delving into a game-changing technique – The Infinity Technique. It's not just about speed; it's about achieving peace of mind, better focus, and heightened comprehension. As we progress, this technique will not only boost your reading speed by about 25%, but it's also a gateway to smarter reading. Now, let's break it down

  • 1

    Visual Pacer Mastery

    Recap on the visual pacer technique using your finger, an invaluable tool for speed, focus, and preventing regression.

  • 2

    Indentation Technique

    Learn to save time by indenting about one inch from the left and right margins, harnessing the power of peripheral vision.

  • 3

    The Infinity Technique

    Immerse yourself in the Infinity drill, a finger or pen exercise tracing the lazy eight. Slow, deliberate movements stimulate eye health, enhance left-right brain communication, and create a bridge for a richer reading experience.

  • 3

    Integration Challenge

    Connect the Infinity drill to your reading routine. Pick up your book, marked for consistency, and embark on a two-minute reading challenge to test comprehension.

Your Actions for Today

  • Warm up with the Infinity technique before your reading.
  • Read for 10 minutes using the Indentation method (pacing the midsection of the lines).
