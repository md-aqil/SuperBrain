  • Daily Reading Practice:

    Set aside dedicated time each day for reading practice. Aim for at least 10 minutes of focused reading.

  • Consistent Finger Tracking:

    Practice using your finger to guide your eyes while reading. Ensure a smooth left-to-right motion with the indentation.

  • Posture and Breathing:

    Maintain an upright and comfortable posture while reading. Pay attention to your breathing, ensuring it's calm and steady.

  • Infinity Sign Exercise:

    Perform the Infinity sign exercise with your finger regularly. Follow the lazy eights or Infinity sign slowly and deliberately.

  • Daily Incremental Goals:

    Set achievable daily reading goals to track progress. Gradually increase the duration as you become more comfortable.

  • Interrupt Procrastination:

    Recognize procrastination and break tasks into manageable chunks. Overcome open loops by completing what you start.

  • Schedule Reading Time:

    Schedule dedicated reading time in your daily routine. Prioritize this mental exercise just like physical workouts.

  • Variety in Reading Material:

    Explore different genres and topics for a well-rounded experience. Challenge yourself with diverse content to enhance comprehension.

  • Mind-Body Connection:

    Acknowledge the connection between body movement and brain activation. Incorporate exercises that stimulate communication between the left and right brain.

  • Practice Breathing and Mindfulness:

    Integrate deep breathing exercises to enhance focus. Practice mindfulness to stay present and engaged in your reading.

  • Regular Assessment:

    Periodically assess your reading speed and comprehension. Adjust your practice routine based on your progress.

  • Encourage Community Engagement:

    Share your progress and challenges within the community. Support and encourage fellow learners on their quick reading journey.
